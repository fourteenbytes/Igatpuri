History of ITI Igatpuri

पद्मविभूषण सुंदरलाल बहुगुणा

उत्तराखंडच्या टिहरी येथे 9 जानेवारी 1927 रोजी सुंदरलाल बहुगुणा यांचा जन्म झाला होता. त्यांनी केवळ पर्यावरणावरच नाही तर अस्पृश्यतेविरोधातही आंदोलन केलं.


त्यांनी स्त्रियांच्या प्रश्नावरही आवाज उठवला.


गांधीजींपासून प्रेरणा घेऊन त्यांनी 'हिमालय बचाव'चे काम सुरू केलं. आयुष्यभर त्यांनी हिमालयाच्या संरक्षणासाठी आंदोलन केलं. त्यामुळे त्यांना 'हिमालय रक्षक' म्हणूनही संबोधलं जातं. वृक्ष संवर्धनासाठी चिपको आंदोलन सुरू केलं. या आंदोलनाची जगानेही दखल घेतली होती. त्यांच्या समाजकार्याची दखल घेऊन भारत सरकारने त्यांना 1980 मध्ये 'पद्मश्री' आणि 2009 मध्ये 'पद्मविभूषण' पुरस्कार देऊन गौरवलं होतं.

Trending ITI Courses

COPA

COPA full form is Computer Operator and Programming Assistant. The Industrial Training Institute COPA course is all about the training course of computer operation and its programming. Through the ITI COPA course, students gain knowledge by using a computer doing the basic programming of computers.

Machinist

The Machinist course prepares students to work with machine tools, precision measuring instruments, and materials. Students will learn to operate machines such as lathes, mills, and grinders to create high-precision components.

Carpenter

This course teaches students woodworking skills, including the use of hand tools and machines. Students will be trained in constructing wooden structures, furniture, and cabinetry for both residential and commercial projects.

Welder

The Welder course focuses on training students in various welding techniques, including MIG, TIG, and Stick welding. Students will gain hands-on experience in welding materials for construction, repair, and manufacturing.

Plumber

This course provides training in plumbing systems, including the installation and maintenance of pipes, fixtures, and appliances. Students will learn how to work with both water and gas systems in residential and commercial buildings.

Diesel Mechanic

The Diesel Mechanic course focuses on the repair and maintenance of diesel engines used in trucks, buses, and industrial machinery. Students will gain technical knowledge and hands-on skills in diagnosing and repairing diesel-powered vehicles.
